About Us

Creating opportunities and fostering a community where every child can thrive.

Who We Are

Everyone deserves a chance. At SOFI, we strive to provide opportunities for individuals with neurological disorders and a wide array of developmental disabilities. We were founded to do more than meet basic needs—we work to modernise our special ones’ lives. Our programs provide entertainment like gaming consoles, musical instruments, books and other creative outlets to nourish creativity and promote a holistic life.

We aim to give our special ones the freedom to decide what they want to be, regardless of societal neglect. Through judgement‑free community spaces and team‑building activities, we help participants share ideas, connect and empower one another. We collaborate with volunteers and utilise technology to reach underserved communities and stimulate mental and social development.

We also offer mentoring programs that connect young professionals with our special ones to build meaningful relationships and offer guidance. Our services include emotional, physical, financial and mental support, and we educate society about developmental and neurological differences and the profound impact of inclusive support.

Who We Serve

We do not place boundaries on anyone that needs our help. We provide resources and opportunities to all who seek them, irrespective of economic situation or specific diagnosis. Our outreach includes children, adolescents and adults with ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Conduct Disorder, Intellectual Disability, Separation Anxiety Disorder, Tourette Syndrome and psychosis, but we welcome anyone striving to reach their potential. Diagnosis is not required to participate in our programs.
