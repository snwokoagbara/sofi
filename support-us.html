"10 = 1" Project

$10 provides 1 nutritious meal per child. Every day, thousands of children in rural and underserved communities face hunger as a normal part of life. For many, school, play, and even sleep happen on an empty stomach. We believe no child should have to choose between learning and hunger. This project meets an urgent need by providing nutritious, reliable meals to children who would otherwise go without.

Each $10 donation provides one meal, while $5 helps us extend our reach even further. Every dollar goes directly toward sourcing, preparing, and distributing meals to rural communities where access to food is limited. Amplify Our Impact

Beyond providing meals, we harness the power of social media to multiply our impact. Micro food influencers and content creators partner with us to host lunches, share their experiences, and spread the word. Your support helps fund these events and the content that inspires others to join our mission. By giving today, you empower both the children we serve and the community of supporters who make this work possible.

We also welcome volunteers, corporate sponsors, and partners who share our vision of a world where every child is seen, valued, and given the chance to thrive. Reach out to explore partnership opportunities and make a lasting difference.
