Our Mission

We believe no child’s future should be limited by disability or hunger. At the Special Ones Foundation, we support children with developmental differences while providing nourishing meals to underserved children in need. By empowering minds and sustaining bodies, we help every child grow, dream and reach their fullest potential. One meal can restore strength and one act of support can unlock possibility.

Latest Initiative

With just $600 and the help of micro food influencers and content creators, we are organizing community lunches for children who would otherwise go without meals this season. Each creator hosts a local lunch, documenting the experience to raise awareness and inspire donations so we can expand our reach. Every event feeds children and tells a story of hope.
